Until now, I've followed many good habits and discarded many bad habits. But
after some time, I somehow get deviated and forget them. This is why, this time
I would like to record this.

Good Habits - Which I know would impact my life in a good way in the long run
* Waking up early (7AM is the sweet spot)
* Drinking salt water (for electrolytes) (preferably warm water) after waking up
* Make Bed
* Go to Running in the morning
* Do some kind of intense exercises
* Bath in the evening or in the Night (after doing heavy and intense exercise(s))
* Try not to sleep in the day time
* Sleep early in the night (11PM is the sweet spot)
* Do intense meditation in the morning (preferably before eating food)
* Clean the environment of the study place and keep the study area different from the comfort zone and keep it only for study sessions
* Make the food in the morning. Make a list of items that could be made, so I don't have to waste time on deciding that.
* Do the hard stuff first. Don't start the day with some game or YouTube or Instagram or anything else.
* Start the day slowly. It would be hard in the start, but it would pay-off in the long term.
